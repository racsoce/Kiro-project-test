# Product Overview

RACSOCE needs a centralized system to manage customer data across their growing business. Currently, customer information is scattered across spreadsheets and legacy systems, making it difficult to maintain data accuracy and provide timely customer service.

## Business Problem

- Customer data is fragmented across multiple systems
- No single source of truth for customer information
- Manual processes lead to data inconsistencies
- Support teams cannot quickly access customer records

## Solution

A serverless customer management API that provides:
- Centralized customer data storage
- Secure access for authorized users only
- RESTful endpoints for integration with other systems
- Scalable architecture that grows with the business

## Target Users

- Customer service representatives
- Sales team members
- Internal applications requiring customer data
